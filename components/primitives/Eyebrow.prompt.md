Mono uppercase meta label; sits above headings, in tickers, and as section markers.

```jsx
<Eyebrow dot="accent">{["Portfolio, 2026", "Selected work"]}</Eyebrow>
```

Array children are joined with middots. `dot="accent"` puts an orange dot in front, `dot="live"` a green one (use for the NOW ticker only). `accent` colors the text orange.
