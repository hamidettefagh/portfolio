Large contact row for the contact section; text links only, no social icons.

```jsx
<ContactLink label="Email" value="hamid.ettefagh@gmail.com" copyable />
<ContactLink label="LinkedIn" value="in/hamidettefagh" href="https://linkedin.com/in/hamidettefagh" />
```

`copyable` makes click copy the value and flash "Copied"; otherwise the row navigates and shows a northeast arrow.
