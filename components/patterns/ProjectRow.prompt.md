```jsx
<ProjectRow index="01" title="Airline super agent program"
  summary="Stabilized a failing go-live and co-authored the next-generation agent roadmap."
  org="Major US airline" href="project.html" />
```

Keep summaries to one sentence. `org` takes industry descriptors only, never client names.
